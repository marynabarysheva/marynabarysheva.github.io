My writing philosophy

My philosophy behind writing is simple. I write what I believe in and believe in what I write. Writing is an honest, emotional dialogue. It resonates. It provokes questions. It intrigues. It makes you act.

My experience with writing

Genetics, military, automobiles,relationships,education. I dare you to name an area I haven't written in yet. With a two-year working experience in content creation and copywriting, I wrote more than 100 texts for US, British, and Canadian B2B/B2C companies. As a chief copyeditor of an online media outlet, I know what it takes to produce a perfect copy. I hunt YOU for grammar accuracy. I chase for YOUR tone. I write from YOUR heart.
